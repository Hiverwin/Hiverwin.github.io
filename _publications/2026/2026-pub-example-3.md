abstract: >-
  This paper studies Natural Language to Excel Formula generation through the lens of near-miss errors: formulas that parse correctly but fail at execution because of the wrong function, operator, or reference. It introduces an AST-based error taxonomy and the ECFL framework, which uses typed error supervision during training and targeted contrastive retrieval during inference, improving Exact Match by 6.4 points over standard supervised fine-tuning while matching SC@5 at lower inference cost.
